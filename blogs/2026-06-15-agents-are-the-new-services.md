The article argues that agents differ fundamentally from traditional services due to non-deterministic execution paths, variable token costs, shifting model dependencies, and governance challenges around the data flowing through them. Pydantic Logfire addresses these needs with unified observability across LLM calls and infrastructure, including an AI Gateway, evals, guardrails, and new views for services, Kubernetes, hosts, and metrics shipping this week.
