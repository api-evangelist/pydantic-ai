This article introduces Pydantic Logfire's new Service Inventory and topology graph, which help identify bottlenecks across microservices. In a Black Friday scenario, a LangGraph workflow calling 14 services experiences 8% request timeouts, and the feature pinpoints the slow dependency in three clicks by visualizing service dependencies and their performance metrics.
