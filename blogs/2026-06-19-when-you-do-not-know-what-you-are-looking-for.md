A CrewAI multi-agent workflow that previously took 90 seconds now requires four minutes, with no obvious errors or alerts. This article introduces Pydantic Logfire's new Metrics Explorer, which lets developers browse metrics in three steps - pick a namespace, pick a metric, and see what you can break it down by - with no SQL required.
